These clubs provide students with opportunities to enhance their technical skills, contribute to social causes, develop leadership qualities, participate in sports, and showcase their artistic talents. Technical clubs like Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC), CodeChef, and IEEE help students gain hands-on experience in coding, app development, and emerging technologies. Social and welfare-oriented clubs focus on community service, environmental sustainability, and spreading awareness on important issues. Cultural clubs encourage participation in dance, music, drama, and literary events, while sports clubs promote fitness and competitive spirit. These clubs not only allow students to pursue their passions but also help in networking, skill-building, and overall personality development.</big></p>
