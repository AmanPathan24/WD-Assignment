Vishwakarma Institute Of Technology

Where Dreams Took Shape, and Vision Became Reality
From Humble Beginnings to an Unstoppable Legacy

Vishwakarma Institute of Technology (VIT), Pune, established in 1983, is one of the premier engineering institutes in Maharashtra. Affiliated with Savitribai Phule Pune University and accredited by NBA and NAAC, VIT is known for academic excellence, innovation-driven education, and a student-centric approach.

VIT Pune offers a vibrant ecosystem of clubs, cells, and student-run organizations that foster holistic development beyond the classroom:

✅ Technical & Innovation Clubs - VIT boasts a wide range of technical clubs like CodeChef VIT, IEEE Student Chapter, and SAEINDIA Collegiate Club. These platforms promote innovation, hands-on projects, hackathons, and technical competitions.

✅ Cultural & Performing Arts Clubs - From drama to dance, music to literature, VIT's cultural clubs like Kala, Swar, Nrityam, and Dramatics Club offer students a creative outlet and build confidence through fests and intercollegiate events.

✅ Entrepreneurship & Start-up Cells - VIT's Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) and Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) nurture entrepreneurial spirit by organizing startup summits, business plan competitions, and networking events.

✅ Social Impact & Leadership Clubs - Through clubs like NSS, Rotaract, and SPDC (Student Personality Development Cell), students engage in community service, leadership training, and social impact initiatives, shaping them into responsible citizens.

✅ Sports & Fitness Clubs - VIT encourages physical well-being and sportsmanship through regular sports events, inter-college tournaments, and fitness awareness programs.
