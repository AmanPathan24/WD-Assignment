CULTURAL CLUBS
AAROH
CLUB
AAROH is VIT’s music club where students with a passion for singing and instrumental music come together to jam, perform, and compose. From classical to contemporary, AAROH provides a space to express emotions through rhythm and melody.
CULTURAL CLUBS
POET
CLUB
The POET Club at VIT nurtures the love for poetry, storytelling, and literature. It encourages students to express their thoughts creatively through open mics, writing sessions, and literary discussions, enriching the campus with artistic expression.

VICULP
CLUB
VICULP is VIT’s official acting and theatre club that celebrates the art of storytelling through drama, stage plays, and expressive performances. It offers students a platform to explore acting, scriptwriting, and stage direction while boosting confidence and creativity.

ZEPHYR
CLUB
ZEPHYR is the official dance club of VIT, known for its energetic performances across styles like hip-hop, classical, freestyle, and fusion. It brings together passionate dancers to collaborate, compete, and captivate audiences with powerful choreography.
